# 常见问题 / FAQ

**Q: 如何开始合作？/ How do we start?**
A: 直接在 Telegram 上联系我们，描述您的需求，我们会安排专人跟进并提供免费方案咨询。
Contact us on Telegram, describe your needs, and we'll assign someone to follow up with a free consultation.

---

**Q: 开发周期多长？/ How long does development take?**
A: 取决于项目规模。小型项目通常 3–7 天，中型 2–4 周，大型按里程碑排期。
Depends on scope. Small projects: 3–7 days; Medium: 2–4 weeks; Large: milestone-based schedule.

---

**Q: 支持修改和售后吗？/ Do you provide revisions and after-sales support?**
A: 是的，项目交付后提供一定期限的免费 bug 修复，具体保修期在合同中注明。
Yes. We provide a free bug-fix warranty period after delivery, specified in the contract.

---

**Q: 源码会交付给我吗？/ Will source code be delivered?**
A: 根据合同约定，可提供完整源码交付。
Source code delivery is available as agreed in the contract.

---

**Q: 数据安全有保障吗？/ Is data security guaranteed?**
A: 我们严格保护客户数据，签署保密协议（NDA）视需求而定。
We protect client data strictly. NDA signing is available upon request.

---

**Q: 能否接急单？/ Can you handle urgent orders?**
A: 可以，加急项目可优先排期，但可能涉及加急费用，请提前说明。
Yes, urgent projects can be prioritized, possibly with an expedite fee. Please specify upfront.

---

**Q: 你们团队在哪里？/ Where is your team based?**
A: 我们是远程团队，主要成员位于中国大陆、东南亚，支持全球客户。
We are a remote team with members in mainland China and Southeast Asia, serving global clients.
